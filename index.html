Birds Often Seen in Lincoln Heights, Los Angeles

I've been lucky enough to see these birds in my backyard. I've included some fun facts about each bird.

California Scrub Jay

Fun fact: Scrub jays have been observed gathering around dead jays, creating a cacophonous "funeral" that can last for up to half an hour.

California Towhee

Fun fact: The California Towhee looks for food by dragging its feet through leaves and twigs with a distinctive two-legged backward hop.

Red-Tailed Hawk

Fun fact: diving speed of up to 120 mph and excellent vision, seeing 8 times better than humans.

Common Raven

Fun fact: Ravens are highly intelligent. They can recognize and remember human faces and even using tools to find food.

Ruby-Throated Hummingbird

Fun fact: Hummingbirds have remarkable spatial memory, allowing them to remember feeder locations years in advance.

White-Throated Swift

Fun fact: Swifts can can sleep on the wing and even mate while airborne, spending most of their lives in the air.
